Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

The short version: FLIPT was built to escape surveillance, not create it. We collect as little as possible and never sell your data.

1. Our Philosophy

I escaped from a system designed to track every purchase and link it to your identity. I'm not about to build the same thing. Privacy isn't a feature — it's the foundation.

2. What We Collect

Waitlist signups:

  • Email address (only if you choose to join)
  • Timestamp of signup

We do NOT collect:

  • Your real name or identity
  • Browsing history
  • Location data
  • Device fingerprints
  • Wallet addresses (until you choose to connect)

3. How We Use Data

Email addresses are used only to:

  • Notify you when FLIPT launches
  • Send important updates (rare)

We will never sell, rent, or share your email with third parties.

4. Blockchain Transactions

FLIPT operates on public blockchain infrastructure. Transactions are visible on-chain by design. However:

  • We don't link wallet addresses to personal identities
  • We don't track your activity across platforms
  • You control your own keys and data

5. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for basic functionality only. No tracking cookies. No ad networks. No analytics that identify you.

6. Third Parties

We don't share data with third parties except:

  • Infrastructure providers necessary to run the service
  • Legal requirements (if compelled by law)

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Request deletion of your email from our waitlist
  • Ask what data we have about you (spoiler: just your email)

Email davidflip25@proton.me for any requests.

8. Security

We use encryption and security best practices. But no system is perfectly secure. Use strong passwords and protect your own wallet keys.

9. Changes

If we change this policy, we'll update this page. Major changes will be announced.

10. Contact

Questions about privacy? davidflip25@proton.me

I'm David. I chose freedom. — Flip it forward.
